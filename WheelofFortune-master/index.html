Instructions

Spin the wheel, guess a letter, and solve the puzzle!

Try to earn as many points as you can through 10 rounds.

If you need a little help, you can get a hint for 200 points or skip the round.

Be careful ... wrong guesses will cost you 500 points and BANKRUPT will send your round score back to zero!
